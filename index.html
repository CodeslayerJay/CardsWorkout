BodyCore

BodyCore is a simple random workout generator using bodyweight exercises. The concept comes from using a deck of playing card to create workouts. Each suit in the deck represents an exercise--Spades: Squats, Hearts: Pushups, Diamonds: Pikes, Clovers: Crunches. The number on the card represents how many reps to perform with face cards being 10 reps.

How to do the workouts:

Perform as many exercises with perfect form in the fastest time possible. When you are finished note how many exercises you completed by the total exercise count in top right corner of screen and the current duration of workout. If you want a greater challenge, then select a more difficult workout plan or try to beat your previous workout count and duration.

Example workout plan:

Mon: Lower Body
Tues: Full Body
Wed: Core & Abs
Thurs: Full Body
Fri: Upper Body
Sat: Rest / Optional
Sun: Rest / Optional
